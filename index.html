hi!

I am a first year CS PhD student at UT Austin, advised by Dr. Keshav Pingali at the Center for Distributed and Grid Computing at the Oden Institute.

My research focuses on deep reinforcement learning, particularly multi-agent learning and exploration in sparse-reward settings. I'm also interested in RLHF and evolutionary methods.

During my undergraduate years I worked at the intersection of cybersecurity and machine learning, and I'm still broadly interested in cybersecurity. I completed my MS thesis on hybrid evolutionary RL algorithms under the supervision of Dr. Keshav Pingali and Dr. Risto Miikkulainen at UT Austin.

I am from Ankara, Turkey. You might also know me as Zelal.
